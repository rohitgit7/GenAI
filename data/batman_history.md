# Batman: The Dark Knight

**Batman** is a fictional superhero appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics.

## Profile
*   **Real Name:** Bruce Wayne
*   **Alias:** The Caped Crusader, The Dark Knight
*   **Base:** Gotham City
*   **Affiliations:** Justice League, Bat-Family

> "I am vengeance. I am the night. I am Batman!" — Batman: The Animated Series

## Key Abilities
1.  Genius-level intellect
2.  Master detective
3.  Expert martial artist
4.  High-tech equipment (Batmobile, Batarangs)
